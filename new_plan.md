# How to Use This Plan

**For the AI Tutor:**
- Each day is designed for ~60 minutes of study
- Teach the grammar concept with examples, then drill with exercises
- Introduce vocabulary in context, use images/scenarios where possible
- Speaking exercises should use speech-to-text; correct pronunciation
- Writing exercises: user writes, AI reviews for grammar and content
- Refreshers should be quick (5-10 min) ‚Äî quiz format works well
- End each day with a short review of new material

**Daily Structure:**
1. Grammar (~20 min): Explain concept, examples, quick drill
2. Vocabulary (~20 min): New words in context, example sentences
3. Combined Exercise (~20 min): Use both grammar + vocab together
